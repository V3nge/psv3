Star Wordle Game

Star Wordle is a new version of Wordle for Star Wars fans. In this game, you will be tested on your knowledge about galaxies far, far away. All words in Star Wordle are taken from movies, TV programs, comics, novels and video games. Try to guess 5-letter words related to Star Wars in six tries. This is the best way to increase your Star Wars knowledge. May the Force be with you.

How to play Star Wordle

1
Click on any letter at the bottom of the grid to type it into the first empty cell. You should write a valid 5-letter word. Please note: all words must be Star Wars related. Start with “darth” or “vader”, for example.
2
When 5 letters have been typed, press the Enter button.
3
Follow the colored prompts. If you see a green tile, it means that you have chosen the correct letter and put it in the right place. Yellow tiles mean you put the right letter in the wrong place. Is the tile gray? Oops, this letter doesn’t occur anywhere in the word.
4
Try to guess the hidden word using no more than six attempts. May the Force be with you.
